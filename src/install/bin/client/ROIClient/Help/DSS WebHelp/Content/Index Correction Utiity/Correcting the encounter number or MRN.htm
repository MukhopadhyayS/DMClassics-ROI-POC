Correcting the encounter number or MRN

This task tells you how to analyze an expired document or exception document that either has no encounter number or MRN, or has an encounter number or MRN that cannot be found in the OneContent system. These instructions assume you are viewing information for an expired document or exception document that meets this criteria.

  1. Launch the OneContent application and display document information for the patient name that corresponds to the name on the error document.
  1. Check the date of service from the ICU document, and then inspect the OneContent information for an encounter number or MRN with the same date.
  1. If you find a matching date, enter the encounter number or MRN from the OneContent data into the ICU window, then click Update to apply your entry.
  1. If you do not find a matching date and cannot correspond the document to an appropriate encounter number or MRN, follow department procedures established by your facility to resolve this error. For more information, see “About resolving or deleting error documents” earlier in this chapter.
