Overview

The McKesson OneContent Patient Folderä product is an enterprise document management and medical document imaging solution. McKesson OneContent Patient Folder features help organizations overcome the barriers of physical documents and manual processes by consolidating patient chart and record information within a healthcare enterprise.

Important: Annotation and overly note functions are no longer supported by the DSS viewer.  References to annotations and overlays should be ignored.
