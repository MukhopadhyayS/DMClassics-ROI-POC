Customer Support

To report all high and critical service orders, authorized customer personnel can contact the OneContent Support Center at 1-800-800-1844 and follow the prompts . Authorized users should continue to submit standard priority service orders through the Customer Portal™ website.

The Customer Portal™ website offers executive messages, company news, a link to product documentation on Download Central website, detailed product support information, product education course offerings, resources such as white papers, and much more.

The Customer Portal also allows registered users to access an online service order submission and tracking tool. You can submit new service orders and view information about service orders that have been logged in the past. In addition, you can access a knowledge base tool, KNOVA, which provides answers to questions about Allscripts document management products used by your enterprise.

To navigate to the Customer Portal login page and register, go to https://customerportal.mckesson.com in your Web browser and follow on-screen instructions.

Responding to problems

Use the following procedure to report problems to Customer Support and for follow-up attention.

Standard OneContent issues

Step If Then
1 You encounter an issue
  1. If working within OneContent, check the online help.
  2. Check for a solution in the online knowledge base (KNOVA).
  3. Check for a solution in the searchable documentation library available on Download Central.
2 You do not find the solution in step 1 AND you cannot resolve the issue yourself
  1. Determine the number of users affected (one or many).
  2. Make a screen print of the issue (if applicable).
  3. Attempt to duplicate the issue.
  4. Document the necessary steps to reproduce the issue. Include the impact this has on your business operation.
  5. Please make note of any changes that were recently made including application of Microsoft patches, software updates, updates on any systems that interface with OneContent, network changes, workflow changes, and new users or queues.
  6. Collect as much information about the issue as possible from the appropriate application logs.  
  7. Report the error to OneContent Patient Folder Customer Support.
    • To enter service orders online, go to https://customerportal.mckesson.com.
    • To reach the Customer Support Center, call 1-800-800-1844 and follow the prompts.

Note: The Customer Support Manual is available in the OneContent Patient Folder section of the Customer Portal.
3 The problem is determined to be a defect Customer Support notifies OneContent development of the problem and provides you with a tracking number for the defect.
