About resolving or deleting error documents

Documents with a missing or invalid encounter number or MRN can never be uploaded into the system. The document remains in the CAB_HOLD table. Your facility should have a policy or procedure for handling such documents.

Generally, options within the McKesson OneContent Patient Folder environment are:

• Use the Index Correction Utility Delete function to delete the document information from the CAB_HOLD table. See instructions for Deleting documents.
• Assign such documents to a ‘dummy’ encounter. This requires your facility to set up a fake patient account (often called ‘Test Patient’ or ‘ICU Patient’); generally, such accounts use an encounter number or MRN consisting entirely of 9s. You can then assign the document to the dummy encounter or MRN and allow the document to remain there for a time. When you are sure no such patient is actually admitted, you can use the Adjust Document Indexes function at the McKesson OneContent Patient Folder to delete the document.
• Allow such documents to remain in the CAB_HOLD table. This may be appropriate when, for example, it is common practice at your facility for professionals to generate documents for a patient before the patient is actually admitted. After the patient is admitted, you can then use the Index Correction Utility to match a document with the patient’s encounter number or MRN. If no such patient is admitted, you can use the ICU Delete function to dispose of the document.

Contact your supervisor or System Administrator if you are unsure of the procedures used by your facility.
