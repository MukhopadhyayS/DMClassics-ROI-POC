About billing and shipping

When you build a packet of patient records to release, you must enter the number of pages you're releasing and assign billing charges to the packet. You must also specify shipping information, such as the ship-to address and shipping charges. The ROI Module associates this information with the request and allows you to add, change, or delete information as needed until one or more of the documents is released.  

Note: Remember that unsaved changes are discarded if you are automatically logged off the system. To prevent the loss of billing information, McKesson recommends that you periodically save information. Also, the Billing & Payment function is accessible only after you have clicked the Save & Bill button on the Patient Information form to generate billing for the request. (See Release documents for more details.)

Permissions

Your MPF Workstation user profile controls your ability to access and use the ROI functions. If you do not have certain permissions, some buttons and menu options are hidden. If you cannot perform an operation, you most likely do not have appropriate permissions, and you should consult your system administrator.

Billing charges

Billing charges can include one or more billing tiers, fees of various kinds, adjustments, and payments. They also include shipping charges.  

Specifically, billing comprises these artifacts.

  • Billing templates  
    Billing templates developed by your enterprise contain billing tiers, fee types, and related information used for determining the amount to charge for ROI requests.

  • Billing tiers  
    Billing tiers enable your enterprise to charge different rates for different levels of service. The electronic billing tier exists for MPF-based documents and is always available. A Non-MPF billing tier may exist. Other billing tiers can be created as needed by the enterprise.

  • Fees  
    Organizations may charge various sorts of fees.

  • Pre-bills  
    Some requestors are required to pay for service before the requested documents can be released. You can generate a pre-bill letter at any time.

  • Invoices  
    You can generate an invoice at any time.

  • Adjustments
    Adjustments consist of debit and/or credit amounts posted against an ROI request.  

  • Payments  
    Payments for an ROI request can be posted at any time.

  • Shipping charges  
    These charges reflect the cost of delivering the requested records.

 

Shipping

Shipping methods, payment methods, and the cost for shipping ROI requests are defined on the billing template. You can override default shipping information for an ROI request at any time.

The system automatically calculates weight based upon the number of pages released and provides a link (if configured) to the shipper's website. Users enter the weight and destination information at the shipper's website to trigger shipping charge calculations, and then copy the charges to the ROI billing screen.
