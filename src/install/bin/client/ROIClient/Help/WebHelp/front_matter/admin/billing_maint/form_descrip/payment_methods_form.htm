Payment Methods form

The Payment Methods form allows you to view, add, modify, or delete payment methods that users can select when posting payments for ROI requests.  Examples include cash, check, and XYZ credit card.

To open the form

Select the Administration tab > Billing Maintenance > Payment Methods.
