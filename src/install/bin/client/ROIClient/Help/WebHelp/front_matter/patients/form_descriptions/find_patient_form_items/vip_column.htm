VIP column

Indicates that an MPF encounter or non-MPF patient has VIP () status. For MPF encounters, the status is passed from the HIS (ADT) system to the MPF system. For non-MPF patients, the status is set on the Patient Information form.

Not sortable.

VIP status restricts access to a patient's records.  

  • If you do not have VIP permissions, the system hides all non-MPF patients with a VIP status and any MPF patient for whom all encounters have VIP status.

  • If you do not have VIP permissions and the system returns an MPF patient who has a mix of VIP and non-VIP encounters, the system displays only the non-VIP encounters and informs you that VIP encounters exist.

  • If you have VIP permissions, the system displays non-VIP and VIP encounters and patients.
