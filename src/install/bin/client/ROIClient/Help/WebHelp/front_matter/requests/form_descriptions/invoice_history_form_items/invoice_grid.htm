Invoice grid

Lists all invoices associated with the currently selected request.

Initially sorted by Date | Time in most recent to oldest order.

Displays the following invoice information:

  • Date | Time
    The date and time when the invoice was created.
    Format is controlled by the regional settings of your operating system.

  • Type
    Indicates the type of invoice (Invoice or Pre-Bill).

  • Invoice Number
    The number assigned to the invoice by the system.
    Initial sort order is alphanumeric ascending.

  • Invoice Creator
    The name of the person who created the invoice.
    Initial sort order is alphanumeric ascending.
