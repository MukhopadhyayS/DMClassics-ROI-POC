Status box

Allows you to limit the search to only Active or Inactive requestors or to include All requestors.

  • For All, the system finds requestors who have appeared on a request within the last 90 days, regardless of their status

  • For Active, the system finds only those requestors who have appeared on a request within the last 90 days and have a status of Active

  • For Inactive, the system finds only those requestors who have appeared on a request within the last 90 days and have a status of Inactive

Defaults to All.
