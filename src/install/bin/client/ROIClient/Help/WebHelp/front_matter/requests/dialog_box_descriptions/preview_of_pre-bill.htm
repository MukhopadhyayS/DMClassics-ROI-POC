Preview of Pre-Bill

The Preview of Pre-Bill dialog box displays a preview of an invoice you are generating and allows you to select an output method.  

To open the form

  1. From the Billing & Payment form, click Pre-Bill.

  2. At the Pre-Bill dialog box, select a template and click Continue.
