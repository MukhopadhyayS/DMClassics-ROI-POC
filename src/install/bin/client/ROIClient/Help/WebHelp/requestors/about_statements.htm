About statements

Statements provide the transaction activity for a requestor's account in a single document, allowing the requestor to view the account balance and outstanding charges. It shows accrued charges across all requests including unapplied payments or adjustments.

Statements are generated:

  • From the Requestor's Account Management screen

  • During the release & invoice process
