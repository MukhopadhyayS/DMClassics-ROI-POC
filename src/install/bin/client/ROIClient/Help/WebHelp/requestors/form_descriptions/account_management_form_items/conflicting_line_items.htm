About conflicting line items

A conflicting transaction occurs when a user does not have patient facility permission to at least one of the documents charged to a given invoice.  

This line item is read-only to that user. It is shown in red and a help tip is displayed explaining why the line item is restricted.  The user cannot view the invoice, apply payments or make adjustments to the conflicting line item.

If there are conflicting items such as invoices, unapplied payments, unapplied adjustments, and refunds, the amounts are included in the total charges and account balance.
