Date Range

Specifies the time span for the report. Based upon invoice closed date.

Select from the following options:

  • Today's Date

  • Custom Date.  If selected, enter the applicable dates in the State Date and End Date fields.

  • Current Week

  • Current Month

  • Previous Month

  • 3 Months Prior

  • 12 Months Period

  • Year to Date

  • Previous Year
