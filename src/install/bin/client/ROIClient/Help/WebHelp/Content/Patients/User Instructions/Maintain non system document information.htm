Maintain non-system document information

This task enables you to add, change, or delete information about non-system documents (that is, non-OneContent documents). You can maintain non-system documents for both OneContent and non-OneContent patients. You can add, change, or delete information for only one document at a time.

A non-system document resides outside of the OneContent and ROI system, but billing charges must be captured if it is released to a requestor. The information you enter is used to generate the billing charges.

Note: You can also create or edit non-system documents from the Requests > Patient Information form. See Add or edit non-MPF document information for instructions.

To create non-OneContent documents

  1. Find a patient.
  2. Go to the Patient Records form and click New Document.
  3. On the Create New Non-MPF Document dialog box, enter document information.
  1. Click Save.

To change non-OneContent documents

When you change information for a non-system document, the system automatically applies the updates to any request that includes the document. Once a non-system document has been released on any request, you can edit only the comments associated with the document. This ensures the integrity of the Accounting of Disclosure reports.

The document tree on the Patient Records form lists all encounters, but filters documents based on your record view permissions and on the document types associated with each record view.

  1. Find a patient.
  2. Go to the Patient Records form, and double-click the non-system document in the document tree.
  1. On the Edit Non-MPF Document dialog box, change or enter information as needed.
  2. Click Save.

To delete non-system documents

Note: You cannot delete information for a non-system document that has been released.

  1. Find a patient.
  2. Go to the Patient Records form, and double-click the non-system document in the document tree.
  3. On the Edit Non-MPF Document dialog box, click Delete.
  4. At the confirmation prompt, click Delete.
