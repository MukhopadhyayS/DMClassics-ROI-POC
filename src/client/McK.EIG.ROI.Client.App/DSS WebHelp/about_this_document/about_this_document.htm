Purpose

This document provides user instructions for the OneContent WinDSS Display Workstation (WinDSS) viewer.

Scope

The WinDSS viewer is launched by the Index Correction Utility (and the Release of Information (ROI) Module).

This document describes all WinDSS functionality available when the viewer is launched from any component.

Audience

This document is intended for end-users who view document images.

This document assumes that users have knowledge of OneContent WinDSS software components, are familiar with Microsoft® Windows® user interface principles and terms, and understand the basics of operating a mouse.

Certified Modular Electronic Health Record

The OneContent™ Patient Folder product is a Certified Modular Electronic Health Record (Drummond Group certification 03242011-1135-8) that manages the capture, indexing and completion processes for health information management. It allows customers to gain maximum efficiencies in the deficiency completion process for encounter-based medical documentation. Further, the product allows customers to determine and manage their legal health record defined for disclosure and release of information in compliance with HIPAA and other government regulations and guidelines. It is intended to support clinical decision-making, to capture and query information relevant to health care quality and to exchange electronic health information with, and integrate such information from other sources.

The OneContent™ Business Folder application is a component of the overall product portfolio and is designed to assist patient financial services (commonly called the Business Office) with the management of transactional data and documents relevant to the billing process. It supports the automatic processing of ANSI 4010/5010 x.12 835 transactions representing explanation of benefit information about claims. It allows customers to define the documents that they collect for their business office operations, indexes them to the respective patient encounter and provides inter-departmental workflow to process these documents in a meaningful way. It is not intended to be a solution or module for Electronic Health Records. It is not intended to support clinical decision-making nor physician order entry. It is not intended to capture nor query information relevant to health care quality, and it is not intended to exchange electronic health information with, and integrate such information from other sources.
