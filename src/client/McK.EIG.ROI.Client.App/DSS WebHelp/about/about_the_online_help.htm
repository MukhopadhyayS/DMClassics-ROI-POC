About the online help

Overview

Note: This information is effective with the OneContent™ family of products except where specifically noted. In addition, readers should assume that information for the OneContent™ product is generally applicable to the OneContent™ Business Folder product as well. For easier readability, such passages may cite only "OneContent."

Purpose

The online help provides user instructions for the OneContent WinDSS Display Workstation (WinDSS) viewer.

Scope

The WinDSS viewer is launched by the Index Correction Utility (and the Release of Information (ROI) Module).

The online help describes all WinDSS functionality available when the viewer is launched from any component.

Audience

The online help is intended for end-users who view document images.

This information assumes that users have knowledge of OneContent WinDSS software components, are familiar with Microsoft® Windows® user interface principles and terms, and understand the basics of operating a mouse.
