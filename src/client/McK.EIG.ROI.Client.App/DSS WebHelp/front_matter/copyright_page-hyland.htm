Customer Support

To obtain OneContent™ customer support:

1. Log in to the Hyland Community website (https://community.hyland.com).
2. On the menu bar, do either of the following:
- Click the Customer Portal tab, and then click the Support Issues tab.

OR

- Click the Support tab.
3. Click the Create Support Issue button.
4. In the resulting window, select OneContent as the System Type.
5. Complete and submit the form.
