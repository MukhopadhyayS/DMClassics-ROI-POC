Account Management form

The Account Management form displays invoices (charges), unapplied adjustments, unapplied payments, refunds and pre-bill requests for a requestor's account.

The system displays transactions based on facilities associated with your user profile.

You can perform the following:

  • Manage payments
  • Manage adjustments
  • Manage pre-bills
  • Generate statements
  • Issue refunds

To open the form

Select Requestors > Account Management.

Item descriptions

Account Register grid

Contains the following information:

  • Date
    The transaction date. For invoices, this is the creation date.  For unapplied payments/adjustments and refunds, this is the date the transaction was initiated.
  • Type
    The type of event.  Types include Open/Closed Invoice, Unapplied Payment, Unapplied Adjustment, and Refund.
  • Description
    The description of the event.  Invoices show the invoice number.  Payments, adjustments and refunds show a note.
  • Request ID
    The system-assigned ID of the request.
  • Charges
    The amount of the invoice before adjustments and payments are applied.
  • Adjustments
    The value/amount of the adjustment. Negative values are shown in ( ).
  • Payments
    The value/amount of the payment. Negative values are shown in ( ). Refunds show as a positive value.
  • Pre-bill
    The value/amount of the pre-bill. Negative values are showin in ( ).
  • Billable
    Specifies if the request is billable. A checkmark indicates applicable invoices.
  • Balance
    The total charges minus payments and adjustments.

View

Is enabled when a line item is selected.  

Click to view the details for the selected line item. The system opens the appropriate form: Invoice, Payments, Adjustments, Statements or Refund.

Total Charges

The total of all charges. Includes conflicting transactions.

Total Payments and Adjustments

The total of all payments and adjustments.

Unbillable Amount

The total amount that is unbillable.

Account Balance

The total amount due when applying charges, adjustments, and payments. Includes conflicting transactions.
