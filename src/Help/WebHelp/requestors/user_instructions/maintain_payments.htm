Maintain payments

This task enables authorized users to add, edit, and remove payments.

To add a payment

  1. Find a requestor.
  2. Select Requestors > Account Management.
  3. In the Account Management form, click Add Payment.  The system displays the Payments dialog box.
  4. Enter the payment details.
  5. Click Save.

To edit a payment

You can only edit applied payments.

  1. Find a requestor.
  2. Select Requestors > Account Management.
  3. In the Account Management form, open an invoice to edit. The system displays the Payments dialog box.
  4. In the Payments dialog box, click the red   to delete the payment.  
  5. In the Apply field, type the correct payment amount.
  6. Click Save.

To remove a payment

You can only remove unapplied payments.

  1. Find a requestor.
  2. Select Requestors > Account Management.
  3. In the Account Management form, open the unapplied payment to remove. The system displays the Payments dialog box.
  4. Click Remove Payment.
