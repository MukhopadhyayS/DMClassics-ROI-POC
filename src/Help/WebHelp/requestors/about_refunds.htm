About refunds

Authorized users can issue a refund to a requestor's account when the requestor balance is less than zero and there are unapplied payments (greater than $0).  Refunds can only be applied to unapplied payments.

Refunds are issued for payments only. A refund cannot be issued for an adjustment.

Note: If an overage on a payment or adjustment occurs, modify the payment or adjustment instead of issuing a refund.  The refund process is designed to be used when requestors want to zero out their accounts.
