Requestors

About requestors

Requestors are entities who have requested or who will potentially request copies of patient records. Patients as well as outside agencies can request copies of records, and each requestor must be on file.

The Requestors tab allows you to search for a requestor, access requestor information and request history. If you have proper security permissions, you can also add, change, or delete information for requestors, and create requests (otherwise, buttons and menu options for these functions are hidden).

In addition, the system automatically navigates to Requestor tab functions when:

  • You are required to select a requestor while adding or changing a request.
  • You choose to change requestor information while adding or changing a request.
  • You choose to add a requestor while adding or changing a request.
Access to requestor records

Your OneContent user profile controls your ability to access and use the ROI functions. If you do not have certain permissions, some buttons and menu options are hidden. If you cannot perform an operation, you most likely do not have appropriate permissions, and you should consult your system administrator. If you are logged into ROI when rights are granted, you must log out and log back in for the change to take effect.

Auditing

Auditing is controlled by OneContent settings. If the corresponding codes are enabled in OneContent, the system audits these events:

  • Adding requestors
  • Changing information for an existing requestor (code 2 must be enabled)
