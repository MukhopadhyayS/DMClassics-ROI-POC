Refund dialog box

The Refund dialog box enables you to refund a credit balance on a requestor's account.  

Refunds cannot be issued to accounts that have open invoices.

To open the form

  1. Find a requestor.
  2. From the left pane, select Account Management.
  3. In the Account Management form, click Refund.

Item descriptions

Amount

Specifies the amount of the refund.

Note

Specifies the reason for the refund.

Date

Indicates the date of the refund.  Pre-populated with today's date. Click the drop-down arrow to view the calendar and select a date.

Save

Saves your entries.

Save + Output letter

Saves your entries and opens the Output Letters and/or Statements dialog box.

Cancel

Cancels any unsaved entries and closes the dialog box.
