Sales Tax workflow

1. In the Billing Tiers form, select the Sales Tax option.
2. In the Fee Types form, select the Sales Tax option.
3. In the Billing Location form, select the Billing Location and enter the applicable Tax Percentage. (Define this as a percentage, not the coin conversion - for example, 7 for 7%, not .07).
4. Create a new request.
5. From the menu, select Requests > Release & Invoice.
6. In the Release & Invoice form, do the following:
a. Select a Billing Location.
a. Click the Sales Tax tab, and select Apply Tax. The Electronic fee (if configured) and any other fees are added together and then the tax is applied.
