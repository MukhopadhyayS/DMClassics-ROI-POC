About release and invoice

When you build a packet of patient records to release, you enter the number of pages you're releasing and assign billing charges to the packet. You also specify shipping information, such as the ship-to address and shipping charges, and sales tax if applicable. The ROI Module associates this information with the request and allows you to add, change, or delete information as needed until one or more of the documents is released.  

By default, the system generates invoices for released documents. For eligible requestors, you can choose to release documents without generating an invoice. The requestor type must be configured for optional invoices.

Note: Remember that unsaved changes are discarded if you are automatically logged off the system. To prevent the loss of billing information, Hyland recommends that you periodically save information. Also, the Release & Invoice function is accessible only after you have clicked the Save & Bill button on the Patient Information form to generate billing for the request. (See Release documents for more details.)

Permissions

Your OneContent user profile controls your ability to access and use the ROI functions. If you do not have certain permissions, some buttons and menu options are hidden. If you cannot perform an operation, you most likely do not have appropriate permissions, and you should consult your system administrator.

Billing charges

Includes document charges, fee charges, and custom fees.

Output

An output method is selected for releasing documents. Options include printing, faxing, emailing, or saving as a file.

When printing, the shipping information is provided and shipping charges are calculated. The system automatically calculates weight based upon the number of pages released and provides a link (if configured) to the shipper's website. Users enter the weight and destination information at the shipper's website to trigger shipping charge calculations, and then copy the charges to the ROI billing screen.

Sales Tax

Billing locations can have sales tax associated with them.  A percentage is set and when this billing location is selected, the sales tax is applied based upon the billing tier, fee type, and requestor type.  The user has the option to override the sales tax.
