Maintain adjustment reasons

To add, modify, or delete reasons available to users for adjusting the balance due for an ROI request, do the following:

  1. Access the Adjustment Reasons form.
  2. Follow the instructions below for the task you want to perform.

To add a new adjustment reason

  1. In the main content pane, click Create New.
  2. In the detail pane, enter a Reason Name and Display Text for the adjustment reason.
  3. Click Save.

To modify an existing adjustment reason

  1. In the main content pane, select the adjustment reason you want to modify.
  2. In the detail pane, change the information as needed.
  3. Click Save.

To delete an adjustment reason

  1. In the main content pane, select the adjustment reason you want to delete.
  2. Click Delete.
  3. In the confirmation message, click Delete.
