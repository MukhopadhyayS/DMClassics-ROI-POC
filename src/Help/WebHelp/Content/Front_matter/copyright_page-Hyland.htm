Customer Support

To obtain OneContent™ Customer Support:

1. Log in to the Hyland Community website at https://community.hyland.com.

2. On the main page, do one of the following:

  • Click the Customer Portal menu option, and then click the Support Issues tab.

  • Click the Support menu option.

3. On the displayed page, click the Create Support Issue button.

4. On the displayed page, in the System field click the drop-down list and select OneContent.

5. Complete and submit the form.
