Find Patient dialog box

The Find Patient dialog box allows you to enter search criteria for finding patient information.

It is accessed by clicking Select a Patient on the Accounting of Disclosure Report form.

Item descriptions

Main content pane

Last Name

Last name of the patient you want to find.

You must enter at least the first three letters, or you can enter a total of three letters for the first and last names combined.

First Name

First name of the patient you want to find.

You must enter at least the first three letters, or you can enter a total of three letters for the first and last names combined.

Date of Birth

Date of birth of the patient you want to find. Allows you to enter date of birth in mm/dd/yyyy format.

Providing the date of birth alone is not sufficient information for finding a patient. You must enter information in at least one additional field.

SSN

Social Security Number of the patient you want to find.

You must enter at least the first three numbers.

Facility

The facility where the patient care occurred.

Choosing All Facilities provides results from any and all facilities where patient care occurred.

Providing a facility alone is not sufficient information for finding a patient. You must enter information in at least one additional field.

Enc/Acct

Encounter or Account number of the patient you want to find.

You must enter at least the first three characters.

MRN

Medical Record Number of the patient you want to find.

You must enter at least the first three characters.

Find Patient

Initiates a patient search based on the information you provide.

Reset

Clears all fields of information you entered.

Detail pane

Patient Information grid

Displays the Patient Name and associated information for all patients matching the search criteria. The grid title is preceded by the number of patients found.

Except for the Locked and VIP columns, you can sort on any column by clicking the column header and sort in reverse order by clicking the column header again.

The Patients grid contains the following columns:

  • Locked
    An icon indicates patients that are locked or that have an encounter that is locked. If locked and you do not have security rights to view this information, the system displays the patient's name in the report, but masks all other patient-related or encounter-related information. This information comes from the HIS system.
  • VIP
    An icon indicates patients that have VIP status. If you do not have security rights to view this information, the system displays the patient's name in the report, but masks all other patient-related information.  This information is passed from the HIS system to the system.
  • Patient Name
    First and last names of patients matching the search criteria.
  • Date of Birth
    Date of birth of patients matching the search criteria.
  • SSN
    Social Security Numbers of patients matching the search criteria.
  • Facility
    Facilities where the patient care occurred for patients matching the search criteria.
  • MRN
    Medical Record Numbers of patients matching the search criteria.

OK

Copies information for the selected patient to the Patient Information section of the Accounting of Disclosures form.
