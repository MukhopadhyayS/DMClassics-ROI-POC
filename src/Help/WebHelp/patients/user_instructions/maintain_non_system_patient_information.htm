Maintain non-system patient information

This task tells you how to maintain information for an existing non-system patient. You can view information for both OneContent Patient Folder and non-OneContent Patient Folder patients, but you can add, change, or delete information only for non-OneContent Patient Folder patients.

Access to VIP and/or locked records is controlled by explicit permissions. If you do not have the permissions, the system omits the VIP and/or locked records and displays an alert.

To add or change patient information

You can modify non-OneContent Patient Folder patient information only if the record is not locked.

  1. Find a patient.
  2. On the Patient Information form, edit or enter demographic information as needed.
  3. Click Save.

To delete a patient

You can delete a non-OneContent Patient Folder patient only when the patient is currently not included on a request and no non-OneContent Patient Folder documents have been released for that patient.

  1. Find a patient.
  2. On the Patient Information form, click Delete Non-MPF Patient.
  3. At the confirmation prompt, click Delete Patient.
