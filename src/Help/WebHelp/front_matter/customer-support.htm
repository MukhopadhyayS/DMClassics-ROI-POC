Customer Support

If you have questions regarding the product described in this documentation, contact your system administrator. Authorized users (such as an administrator) can contact the McKesson OneContent™ Support Center at 1-800-800-1844 and follow the prompts to report all high and critical service orders. Authorized users should continue to submit standard priority service orders through the McKesson Customer Portal™ website.

The McKesson Customer Portal™ website offers executive messages, company news, a link to product documentation on McKesson's Download Central Web site, detailed product support information, product education course offerings, resources such as white papers, and much more.

The McKesson Customer Portal also allows registered users to access McKesson’s online service order submission and tracking tool. You can submit new service orders and view information about service orders that have been logged in the past. In addition, you can access McKesson's Knowledge Base tool, which provides answers to questions about McKesson products used by your enterprise.

To navigate to the McKesson Customer Portal login page and register, go to https://customerportal.mckesson.com in your Web browser and follow on-screen instructions.
