View encounter details

This task enables you to view details for all encounters associated with a specific patient. Encounter details include the patient MRN, admit and discharge dates, type of hospital service rendered, discharge disposition, the patient's financial class, and the total current balance for the encounter.

Note: Your site may have defined custom fields for site-specific information at the patient and encounter level. If custom data exists and you do not have permission to view it, the system displays a message. To view custom fields, you must have the View Custom Info permission enabled on your MPF Workstation user profile. To be able to edit custom field values, you must have the Custom Info Maintenance permission.

All information is view only.

  1. Find a patient.

  2. Go to the Patient Records form and click Enc. Details.

  1. Click Close to return to the calling form.
