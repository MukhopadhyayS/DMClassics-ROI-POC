To enter requestor information

On the Requestor Information form:

  1. Enable the prepayment and/or certification letter flags, when appropriate. You may need to consult your supervisor to determine whether these options should be enabled for the requestor you are creating.

  2. For patient requestors:  

    1. The system pre-populates the screen with patient information and splits the Name box into Last Name and First Name. It also displays the Patient EPN field if your system is EPN-enabled.

    2. Enter any missing patient information, such as the patient's EPN (if displayed), DOB, SSN, Facility, and/or MRN.

For non-patient requestors:  

    1. Select the type of requestor you are adding and enter a name.

    2. You can optionally enter the requestor's main address, alternate address, and contact information as desired.
