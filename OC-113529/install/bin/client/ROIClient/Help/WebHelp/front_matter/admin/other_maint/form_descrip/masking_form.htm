Masking form

The Masking form allows you to enable or disable the masking of Social Security Numbers during online ROI sessions and in the reports, letters, and invoices generated by ROI users. When this feature is enabled, all digits of the SSN are replaced with asterisks (*). Otherwise, the entire SSN is visible.

Note: Changing this setting applies only to ROI sessions established after the update. Users that are logged in must log out and log back in for the new setting to take effect.

To open the form

Select the Administration tab > Other Maintenance > Masking.
