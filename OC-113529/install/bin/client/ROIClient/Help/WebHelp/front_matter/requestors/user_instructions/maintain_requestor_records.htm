Maintain requestor records

This task enables authorized users to add, change, or delete requestor records.

 

To add a requestor

See Create a requestor.

 

To change requestor information

  1. Find a requestor.

  2. On the Requestor Information form, change information as needed and click Save.

OR

  1. Find a request.

  2. On the Request Information form, click View / Edit Requestor's Profile.

  3. On the Requestor Information form, change information as needed and click Save.

Note: To discard unsaved changes and set all information to the last saved state, click Revert.

 

To make a requestor inactive

  1. Find a requestor.

  2. On the Requestor Information form, check the Inactive option.

  3. Click Save. The system saves your change and makes all fields read-only, except for the Inactive flag.

 

To delete a requestor

You can delete only those requestors that do not appear on a request. If the requestor is associated with a request, the Delete Requestor button does not display.

  1. Find a requestor.

  2. On the Requestor Information form, click Delete Requestor.

  3. At the confirmation prompt, click Delete Requestor. The system deletes the record and returns you to the Find Requestor form.
