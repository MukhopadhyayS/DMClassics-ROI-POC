About account management

Account management provides a quick and easy process for managing tasks related to a requestor's account including:

  • View an invoice and its related transactions

  • View detailed information about unapplied payments

  • View detailed information about unapplied adjustments

  • View detailed information about refunds

  • Manage payments

  • Manage adjustments

  • Generate statements

  • Issue refunds
