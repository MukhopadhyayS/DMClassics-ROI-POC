About adjustments

Authorized users can post one or more adjustments to a requestor's account and individual invoices.  

  • Adjustments can be posted both before and after documents are released.  

  • Multiple adjustments can be made using a variety of payment types: check, cash, credit card.  Each of these payments types must be posted separately.  

  • Adjustments can be added to an account that has a zero balance or credit.

  • Adjustments can be deleted only when unapplied.  

Adjustments can be applied in any of the following ways:

  • Manually to one or more invoices

  • Manually to the requestor's account

  • Automatically to one or more invoices

Adjustment

An adjustment can be applied to a single invoice, or distributed across multiple invoices. This is an "applied" adjustment.  

Requestor's Account Adjustment

An adjustment can be applied to a requestor's account instead of a specific invoice. This is considered an "unapplied adjustment." This type of adjustment remains in the requestor's account until it is applied.  

Auto Apply

The Auto Apply function allows an adjustment to be distributed across open invoices, with the oldest invoice being paid first.

  • If the adjustment satisfies the invoice balance in full, the invoice balance becomes zero and the invoice is automatically closed.  

  • If the amount of the adjustment exceeds the amount of the open invoice, the next oldest invoice is paid.  

  • The process continues until the adjustment balance is zero or there are no more open invoices.
