Account Management form

The Account Management form displays invoices (charges), unapplied adjustments, unapplied payments, and refunds for a requestor's account.

The system displays transactions based on facilities associated with your user profile.

You can perform the following:

  • Manage payments

  • Manage adjustments

  • Generate statements

  • Issue refunds

To open the form

Select Requestors > Account Management.
