Configure invoice due days

To modify the invoice due days, following the instructions below.

To modify an existing invoice due day

  1. Access the Configure Invoice Due Days form.
  2. In the Invoice Payment Due drop-down list, select the number of days.  Options include 0, 30, 60, 90, 120 or Custom.
  3. If you select Custom, type the number of days in the blank field.
  4. Click Save.
