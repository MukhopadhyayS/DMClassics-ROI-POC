Locked column

Indicates an MPF patient is locked () or has an encounter that is locked.

Not sortable.

Locked status restricts access to a patient's records.

  • If you do not have permission to view locked patients or encounters, you cannot view MPF patients with a locked status (the system does not list those patients).

  • If you do not have permission to view locked patients or encounters and the system finds an MPF patient who has a mix of locked and non-locked encounters, you can view only the non-locked encounters.

  • If you have permission to view locked patients or encounters, you can view all encounters for non-MPF and MPF patients.
