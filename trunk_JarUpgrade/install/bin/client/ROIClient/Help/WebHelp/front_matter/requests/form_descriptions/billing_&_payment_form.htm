Billing & Payment form

The Billing & Payment form enables an authorized user to view, enter, or change billing charges for a currently selected request. If you do not have required permissions, all fields are read-only.

The form also enables an authorized user to generate pre-bill letters and invoices, and to release the requested information.  

You can access the Billing & Payment form only after at least one document has been selected for release on the Patient Information form.

To open the form

  1. Select the Requests tab.

  2. Search for and select a request.

  3. Click View/Edit Request.

  4. Select the Billing & Payment function.

The form displays two tabs:  Billing and Shipping
