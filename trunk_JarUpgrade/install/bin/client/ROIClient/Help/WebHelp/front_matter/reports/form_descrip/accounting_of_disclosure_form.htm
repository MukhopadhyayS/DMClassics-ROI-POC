Accounting of Disclosure form

The Accounting of Disclosure form allows you to generate the Accounting of Disclosure Report. You can select criteria for the report and specify the date range. Security rights are required.

This report is provided at the request of the patient. It reveals the information that was disclosed, to whom it was disclosed, and for what purpose it was disclosed. Patients can have one free report annually. After that, any charges are at the discretion of the facility.

To open the form

  1. Select the Reports tab.

  2. In the Report Name box, select Accounting of Disclosure.  

When the form first displays, the main content pane is blank. To view a report, you must select report options in the left pane and click Run Report.
