Configure Invoice Due Days form

The Invoice Due Days form allows you to view and modify the number of days after generation that an invoice is due.

End-users can override the default invoice due date when creating invoices or when releasing a request in the Release dialog box.

To open the form

Select the Administration tab > Billing Maintenance > Invoice Due Days.

Item descriptions

Invoice Due

Indicates the number of days an invoice payment is due after invoice creation.  Allows you to select a preset value or specify a custom value. To specify a custom value, select custom from the drop-down menu.

Save

Saves the invoice payment due value.

Cancel


Discards any unsaved changes.
